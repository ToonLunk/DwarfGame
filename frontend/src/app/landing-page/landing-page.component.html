Welcome to Dwarf Game!

Dwarf Game (Working Title) is a colony management game where you take control of a company of dwarves and build out a thriving enterprise. You will need to manage your resources, build out your base, and defend against the dangers of the world, including other players! Dwarves are stronger together, so you will need to work with other players to survive and thrive.

This game is currently in open-source development under a custom GPL-3.0 license. If you are interested in contributing to the project through code, art, or design, please join our Discord by clicking here, or view the GitHub repository by clicking here!
